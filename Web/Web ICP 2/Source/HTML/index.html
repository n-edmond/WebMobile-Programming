JOKER




Joker is the silent protagonist of the fifth iteration of the Persona Series. Originally from the countryside, he is forced to move to Tokyo after receiving a criminal record for his attempts to save a woman from a man attacking her. His quiet demeanor belie his strong will, rebellious spirit, and flashy nature. His indomitable allows him to awaken to the power of the Persona and his even more unique ability of the wild card. As a Persona user, became one of the founders of the Phantom Thieves of Hearts .


As leader of the Phantom Thieves, he and his teammates embark to change the hearts of corrupt teachers, leaders, and mentors by entering their palaces and fighting the shadows within. Their goal of reforming society via change of hearts about after the cruelty of their high school teacher that no one was willing to take a stand to. His abuse ran unchecked since the school gained notariaty and fame due to his ability as an ex- olympic medalist.

Personas



Personas are the strength of heart. They are an acceptance of one's faults and negative aspects of onesself they would rather ignore. These aspects do not always have to be negative though. In some cases, one may struggle to accept positive aspects about themselves or doubt their minds. Coming to terms with this can also lead to Persona awakening. Frequently, the awakenings can be painful, and failing to come to terms with it may cause one's shadow to go beserk placing one's life in danger.


The personas themselves belong to the sea of souls. They can become engrossed in strong, distorted desires and lose their identities, forgetting that they belong to everyone.
